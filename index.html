About Migua Lighting & Electricals

At Migua Lighting & Electricals, we are dedicated to providing top-quality lighting and electrical solutions to illuminate your spaces. Our wide range of products is designed to meet the highest standards of safety, efficiency, and aesthetic appeal.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional solutions by leveraging advanced technologies, skilled craftmanship and commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be leading provider of innovative and sustainable electrical solutions in Kenya , driving growth and efficiency through superior products and services.

Our Goal

To be the leading provider of electrical solutions that not only meet but exceed the expectations of customers.

Our Core Values

Quality Innovation Integrity Customer Focus

Our Services

Electrical Products

We offer a comprehensive range of electrical products designed to meet both residential and commercial needs. i.e switches , sockets , circuit breakers , high quality cables , electrical pipes , fittings .

Solar Lighting Solutions

Sustainable and energy-efficient solar lighting solutions i.e solar panels to brighten up your resdential , commercial and industrial spaces while saving on costs.

Powder Coating Services

We provide high-quality professional powder coating services to give your products a durable and aesthetically pleasing finish , custom color matching to meet specific client requirements.

Our store is located along the Eastern Bypass in Utawala, Nairobi. We are easily accessible and offer top-quality lighting and electrical solutions. Visit us for all your needs, and our friendly staff will assist you!
